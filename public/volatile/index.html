Volatile

Volatile is an artwork which consists of two synchronized videos. It juxtaposes the normal and glitched videos, as his other artworks like Another Analogy (2018), Niko no mono ga same space wo occupy suru (2018) and E (2019). This method of juxtaposition is intended to illustrate how the corruptions affect digital data rather than to demonstrate the aesthetics of destruction.

The Volatile was exhibited in the artist's solo show with same title as this work at IAMAS library in 2019. The recorded footage of the exhibition was edited as a video above and exhibited in another group show "Illuminating Graphics 2" in same year.
